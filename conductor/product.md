# Product Definition: Lumina Reader

## Vision
Lumina Reader aims to be the most elegant and immersive EPUB reading experience for desktop users. It bridges the gap between digital convenience and the tactile pleasure of reading, providing a distraction-free environment that prioritizes typography, aesthetics, and accessibility.

## Target Audience
- **Avid Readers / Bookworms**: Individuals who spend hours reading and demand a high-quality, comfortable digital environment.
- **Accessibility-focused Users**: Readers who benefit from specialized fonts (like OpenDyslexic), high-contrast themes, and customizable layouts.
- **General Productivity Users**: Professionals and students who need a clean, modern interface for managing and reading digital books as part of their daily workflow.

## Core Experience
- **Immersive Aesthetic**: Utilizing modern UI trends like glassmorphism and smooth Framer Motion animations to create a "premium" feel.
- **Powerful Management**: Robust tools for organizing large digital libraries using custom collections and smart filtering.
- **Design Excellence**: A relentless focus on refining every UI/UX detail to ensure the interface is both beautiful and functional.

## Strategic Priorities
1. **Design Excellence**: Continuous refinement of the visual language and user interactions.
2. **Library Management**: Implementing custom collections, tags, and advanced search capabilities.
3. **Adaptive UX**: Features that automatically adjust to the user's environment and preferences (e.g., focus mode, dynamic themes).

## Functional Goals
- High-performance EPUB rendering via epubjs.
- Seamless metadata management using a local SQLite database (etter-sqlite3).
- Advanced typography controls and accessibility features.
- Fluid, animated transitions between library and reader views.
